About RugBuster

RugBuster protects crypto investors by identifying rug pulls and scams through real-time token analysis.

Our Mission

RugBuster is dedicated to empowering crypto investors with reliable, accessible tools to avoid scams and rug pulls. Founded in 2025, we provide transparent token analysis and education to help users make informed decisions.

Our goal is to create a safer crypto ecosystem for all.

Our Team

Founded by Micheal Ohagwam (West Africa Fx), RugBuster is built by a team with deep expertise in cryptocurrency and web development, committed to protecting investors.

Our passion drives us to innovate and deliver trusted tools.

Why RugBuster?

  • Free and user-friendly interface for all investors.
  • Powered by trusted APIs like CoinGecko and Etherscan for accurate data.
  • Scalable and secure deployment on Netlify for reliable performance.

We prioritize accessibility, accuracy, and reliability.

How We Help

Our platform offers real-time token analysis, downloadable PDF reports, and educational resources to identify risks such as high holder concentration and unlocked liquidity.
