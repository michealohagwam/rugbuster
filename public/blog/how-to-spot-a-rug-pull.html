How to Spot a Rug Pull

Posted on May 15, 2025 by Micheal Ohagwam | Category: Education | Tags: rug pulls, crypto scams, security

Rug pulls are a common crypto scam where developers abandon a project after raising funds. Here’s how to spot them:

  • Check Liquidity Locks: Use Etherscan to verify if liquidity is locked. Unlocked liquidity is a red flag.
  • Holder Concentration: Avoid tokens where top holders own over 18% of the supply.
  • Team Transparency: Research the team’s background. Anonymous teams are riskier.
  • Audit Reports: Look for audits from reputable firms like CertiK.
